Privacy Policy – Focused Pomodoro Timer

Developer: Sumit Rathore
Contact: sumitmanages@gmail.com

Introduction

Focused Pomodoro Timer is designed to help users manage focus sessions using the Pomodoro technique. Your privacy is important, and this app is built to work completely offline without collecting personal data.

Information We Collect

Focused Pomodoro Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No account creation
  • No personal identifiers collected
  • No access to contacts, location, files, or media

Local Data Storage

The app stores limited information only on your device to function properly:

  • Pomodoro session count
  • Basic app preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

Permissions Used

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

This permission is used only to send timer alerts and session reminders. Notifications are generated locally and are not used for marketing or tracking.

Internet Usage

This app works fully offline and does not use internet connectivity.

Advertising and Analytics

  • No ads
  • No analytics tools
  • No tracking technologies
  • No third-party data sharing

Children’s Privacy

Focused Pomodoro Timer is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
